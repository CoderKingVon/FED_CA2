Sustainability Matters

From Pledges to Progress

SP’s 60-30 Vision is to reduce our greenhouse gas emissions by 60% by 2030, from the baseline year of 2022.

More information can be found in our inaugural Environmental Sustainability Disclosure for Financial Year 2022. We hope this disclosure will serve as a useful reference for other organisations, and see this as fulfilling our calling as one of Singapore’s leading Institutes of Higher Learning

The Plan

Disclosures are for Financial Year 2022, i.e. 1 April 2022 to 31 March 2023. No external assurance has been sought for this disclosure.

We welcome suggestions or feedback to help us improve our subsequent disclosures. You can reach out to SP’s Sustainability Office at sustainability_office@sp.edu.sg.

Our Community In Action

Learn more about SP’s sustainability journey and our collective commitment to reducing impact on the environment we live in. In 2023, we jointly developed The Decarbonisation Playbook for Institutes of Higher Learning (IHLs) with KPMG Singapore. It seeks to share SP’s journey towards decarbonisation so as to be a useful reference for IHLs in their own journey.

Together, as a community, we believe we can achieve our 60-30 Vision by 2030.

Sustainability and Green Skills Courses

SP's Professional & Adult Continuing Education (PACE) Academy has rolled out six new sustainability and green skills courses to add to their existing 33 courses mapped to SkillsFuture Singapore's Green Skills Cluster. The comprehensive course suite spans generating general sustainability awareness, honing cross-sector and cross-functional skills, and equipping specific job functions with sector-specific skills.
